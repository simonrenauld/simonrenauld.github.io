Data Science & Machine Learning

Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Data Science are revolutionizing industries by enabling machines to learn from data, recognize patterns, and make decisions. AI leverages complex algorithms to simulate human intelligence, while Data Science extracts meaningful insights from vast datasets, driving innovation and efficiency across various fields.

LLaMA (Large Language Model Meta AI)

LLaMA is a family of large language models developed by Meta (formerly Facebook). These models are designed for various natural language processing tasks, such as text generation, translation, and summarization. The LLaMA models aim to provide efficient and effective performance while using fewer resources compared to other large-scale models like GPT-3.
