About Cloud Management, Database and Data Warehouse Design

Data management is the process of ingesting, storing, organizing and maintaining the data created and collected by an organization. This is where the database and data warehouse design processes come for the development, implementation and the maintenance of the enterprise data management systems. Properly designed databases are easy to maintain, improve data consistency and are cost-effective in terms of disk storage space and fast to query based on which elements correlate and what data must be stored.
