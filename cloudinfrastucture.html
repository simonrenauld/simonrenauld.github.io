Why Cloud Infrastructure?

Cloud services providers are highly lucrative, making users dependent on providers like Amazon, Google, and Microsoft. Some companies paying $400,000 USD just to extract their own data.

But Why as a individual I need that? Beyong learning new skills, I am building my "Own Knowledge Base", and in some ways, we should all have our own KB.

Why Linux Operating System?

  • Widespread Use: Powers most web servers and cloud platforms.
  • Open Source: Vital for customizing infrastructure.
  • Command Line Proficiency: Enhances adaptability.
  • DevOps & Cloud: Fundamental in workflows and applications.
  • Security: Robust features for securing data infrastructure.

Where to Start?

Fundamentally leaving behind the way I know as individial to learn. So as diverse we are as indivials and available technologies, so as the ways of your learning curves and objectives.

Still have no clue what it's about?

For techies: I'm building my homelab to master cloud infrastructure and all my knowledge in data science while creating a personal knowledge base that promotes independence from cloud providers.

For non-techies: I'm building a low-cost server because I love to learn and keep control of my data and knowledge. This allows me to use personal applications that every non-tech person uses on a daily basis, but with more privacy and control.
