Why Cloud Infrastructure?

Cloud services have become highly lucrative, making users dependent on providers like Amazon, Google, and Microsoft. Extracting your data later can be costly, with some companies paying $400,000 to move their data. Owning or renting physical hardware gives you more control over your data, privacy, and ownership, ensuring a healthier partnership.

Why Linux is Critical

  • Widespread Use: Powers most web servers and cloud platforms.
  • Open Source: Vital for customizing infrastructure.
  • Command Line Proficiency: Enhances adaptability.
  • DevOps & Cloud: Fundamental in workflows and applications.
  • Security: Robust features for securing data infrastructure.
